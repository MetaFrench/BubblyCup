Bubble up your cup

Bubblycup is a new boba tea company that is dedicated to bringing the best and most delicious boba tea to customers around the world. Our company was founded by a pair of passionate boba tea enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of this unique and refreshing drink with others. At Bubblycup, we use only the highest quality teas and ingredients to craft our signature boba tea blends. From classic milk teas to fruity, exotic flavors, we have something for every boba tea lover. And of course, our tapioca pearls are always chewy and perfectly cooked, just the way they should be. In addition to our delicious boba tea, we also offer a variety of toppings and add-ons, so customers can customize their drinks to their liking. Whether you prefer your boba tea sweet, creamy, or fruity, we have the perfect combination for you. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and a welcoming atmosphere at all of our locations. We hope you'll visit us soon and discover the joy of bubblycup boba tea for yourself!
